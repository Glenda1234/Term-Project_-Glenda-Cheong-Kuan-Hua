Kirby and the Forgotten land

Join Kirby on an unforgettable journey

Float off on an all-new adventure as the powerful puffball, Kirby. Explore 3D stages as you discover a mysterious world, save the kidnapped Waddle Dees, and defeat the ferocious Beast Pack. Hope you’re hungry for an unforgettable adventure!

Main Characters

  1. Kirby

    2. A pink and powerful puffball, Kirby can inhale certain enemies and copy their abilities to unleash on others.

  2. Elfilin

    3. After being rescued by Kirby, this mysterious flying creature teams up with our hero to rescue the Waddle Dees.

  3. Badana Waddle Dee

    4. Kirby’s battling buddy who joins the action in 2-player co-op play.

  4. Waddle Dees

    5. Transferred from Planet Popstar, Kirby’s friends have now been captured! Rescue them to help rebuild Waddle Dee Town and unlock more game features.

  5. The Beast Pack

    6. The fierce residents of this strange new world have captured the Waddle Dees—but no one knows why!
